Color Scheme

I chose this color pallete because it had a color similar to the blue in the logo. These shades of blue also give me a feeling of winter.

As you can see I've used these colors throughout the pages. The periwinkle I've used for a background color on the page. Little boy blue has been implimented in the navigation bar. The green blue is used for the headers and footers. Space Cadet is used for hover states in the navigation along with any other accents that will be needed.
