What is a chamber of commerce?

A chamber of commerce is an organization that helps build up local businesses. It is a place for networking and discovering new avenues for advertising. Most importantly it is a local entity populated with local business owners, striving to improve our local economy. In a time of economic uncertainty our goal is to help our neighbor more so than ever before. Help us create a better future for our town. Join us today!
